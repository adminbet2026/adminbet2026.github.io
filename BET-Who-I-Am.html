Who I am

Hi, I'm Yosheika

Therapist, DBT nerd, believer that everyone deserves a space where they don't have to perform.

LCSW, C-DBT · she/her · Brookhaven + virtual across Georgia

The road here

I've been in behavioral health since 2010, and I learned this work in the places where people fight hardest to hold their lives together: community mental health, family and child welfare, K-12 schools, collegiate disability services, and substance use recovery. Long before I had a private practice, I was sitting with people on their hardest days, learning what actually helps.

Those years taught me two things. Healing is possible for everyone. And people from marginalized communities deserve a space to just be themselves, without the judgments of the world trying to define them.

That's the space I built Balanced Embodiment Therapy to be.

What to expect

Sessions with me are warm, real, and yours

I use an eclectic, holistic approach and tailor the care to you: person-centered at the core, with DBT skills, Brainspotting, somatic work, and mindfulness woven in as you need them. I'm a trauma-informed provider, but you don't need a trauma story to belong here. It simply means the care you receive will be informed, intentional, and supportive.

The name is the promise. Keeping balance (Balanced), giving form to feelings and ideas (Embodiment), and healing (Therapy). BET on yourself isn't just our tagline. It's the ask I make of every client, and the bet I make on you from day one.
