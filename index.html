Who I see

You don't have to translate yourself here

I work with adults carrying trauma, identity stress, and the weight of always being “on” in spaces that weren't built for them. The one everyone calls when things fall apart, who has nowhere to put their own. The high achiever who can't stop scanning the room. The person who has spent years explaining themselves and is done explaining.

If you're queer, trans, BIPOC, or all of the above, you won't spend session time teaching me the basics of your life. We start from understood, and we go from there.

How I work

Therapy that works with your whole self

Healing isn't one-size-fits-all, and neither is this. Sessions are warm, real, and built around what you need. Some weeks that's learning concrete skills you can use the same day. Some weeks it's going after the root of what keeps pulling you back. You set the pace, always.

  • DBT skillsConcrete, practiced tools for when emotions are running the show
  • BrainspottingBrain-based processing for trauma that talking alone can't reach
  • Somatic workFor what your body is holding that words can't say yet
  • Person-centeredYour goals lead the work, not a manual and not a diagnosis
